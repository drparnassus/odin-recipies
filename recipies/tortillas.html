Tortillas

Ingredients/Supplies

  • 2 cups: Quaker Harina Preparada White Flour Tortilla Mix
  • 1/2 cup: Warm Water
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Wood Cutting Board
  • Rolling Pin
  • Skillet
  • (Optional) Plastic Wrap or Parchment Paper

Steps/Instructions

  1. Combine 2 cups tortilla mix with 1/2 cup of warm water into a mixing bowl
  2. Mix with hands until the dough is no longer sticking to sides of bowl
  3. Move dough to wooden board and knead it for 5 minutes
  4. Throughout the process, add flour (if needed) to outsides of dough to make it less sticky
  5. Let stand covered for 5 minutes
  6. Seperate dough into balls roughly 2 inches thick, or less thick for thin tortilla style
  7. Press a dough ball flat into the board to make a large circle
  8. Using rolling pin, roll the dough until it reaches desired thickness/size
  9. Lay raw tortillas spaced apart from each other. If you let them dry a bit before stacking, they will stick together less in storage
  10. (alternatively keep them from sticking in storage by placing plastic or parchment between them all)
  11. Heat a skillet to medium heat
  12. Melt butter on the skillet, and add a tortilla. After about 30 seconds, add a little more butter then flip the tortilla
  13. Continue flipping every 20-30 seconds until done
